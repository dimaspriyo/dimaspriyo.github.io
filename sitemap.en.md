### 2. What is ATTUNE — The Framework
**Purpose:** Explain the service — what ATTUNE is and the A-T-T-U-N-E framework

**Content:**
- Section label: "What is ATTUNE"
- Title: "A space to understand yourself"
- Subtitle: "ATTUNE is not just a place to talk. It is a space to understand."
- **The ATTUNE Framework** (acronym cards):
  - **A** — Acknowledge: Understanding your current emotional state
  - **T** — Tell your story: Sharing your experience safely
  - **T** — Trace the pattern: Identifying emotional patterns
  - **U** — Understand: Recognizing needs and triggers
  - **N** — Navigate: Exploring possible directions
  - **E** — Empower: Moving forward with clarity and strength
- **Who it's for:** Relationship challenges, Stress & exhaustion, Family dynamics, Career pressure, Life transitions, Overthinking
- **Session principles:** Confidential, Non-judgmental, Reflection-focused, Professional but warm, By appointment
